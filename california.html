California

California is the most populous U.S. state, known for Hollywood, Silicon Valley, beaches, and diverse climates and cultures.

Famous Places

  • Golden Gate Bridge
  • Yosemite National Park
  • Hollywood

Culture & Food

California food is influenced by Mexican, Asian, and Mediterranean cuisines. It is also home to major wine regions such as Napa Valley.

Technology & Economy

Silicon Valley is one of the world's most important tech centers, home to many major technology companies and startups.
