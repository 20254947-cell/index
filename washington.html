Washington

Washington State is known for its evergreen forests, mountains, and vibrant tech cities.

Famous Places

  • Seattle Space Needle
  • Mount Rainier National Park
  • Olympic National Park
  • Pike Place Market

Culture & Food

Washington has a strong culture of coffee, seafood, and outdoor adventure. The state is known for salmon, Dungeness crab, fresh berries, and its thriving craft coffee scene.

Technology & Economy

Washington is a major technology hub. Seattle is home to large tech companies, startups, and innovation industries. The state also has strong aerospace, manufacturing, and agriculture sectors.
