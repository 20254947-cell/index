Florida

Florida is known for its warm weather, beaches, theme parks, and diverse wildlife. It attracts millions of tourists every year.

Famous Places

  • Walt Disney World
  • Miami Beach
  • Everglades National Park

Culture & Food

Florida's culture is influenced by Caribbean, Latin American, and Southern traditions. Popular foods include Cuban sandwiches, Key lime pie, and fresh seafood.

Technology & Economy

The state is strong in tourism, aerospace, agriculture, and international trade due to its coastal cities and ports.
